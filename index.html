About Me

I am a Texas native but moved to Portland in 2008 to attend Pacific Northwest College of Art. I currently hold a BFA in Intermediate Art with a focus in Animation and Painting. I have worked as an Art Department Intern on IFC's Portlandia where I created the design for Bart Ska-mpson. I also worked on the final season of Hulu's Shrill creating graphics and at Bent Image Labs for an ad campaign for CREA.

I decided to join Epicodus because I've wanted to change my career field for the past few years and find a lot of fulfillment in creating web pages and apps. I recently attended Portland Community College and took multiple classes in Data Analytics and Python Development, and hope to further my education and portfolio with projects that incorporate Machine Learning!
